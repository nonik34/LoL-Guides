# Core Stats

Core stats are the most important numbers that define how a champion performs
in combat and survivability.  

These stats appear on champions, items, and abilities, and they interact with
each other constantly during a match.  

## Health

**Health** determines how much damage your champion can take before dying.  

- When health reaches zero, your champion dies  
- More health allows you to survive longer in fights  
- Tanks and front-line champions usually prioritize health  

## Attack Damage

**Attack Damage (AD)** increases the damage dealt by basic attacks and some
abilities.  

- Mainly used by fighters and marksmen  
- Improves last-hitting minions and sustained damage  

## Ability Power

**Ability Power (AP)** increases the strength of many abilities that deal magic
damage or provide utility.  

- Commonly used by mages and some supports  
- Does not affect basic attacks unless stated  

## Armor

**Armor** reduces damage taken from **physical damage**.  

- Physical damage usually comes from basic attacks  
- Effective against marksmen and many fighters  

## Magic Resist

**Magic Resist (MR)** reduces damage taken from **magic damage**.  

- Magic damage usually comes from abilities  
- Effective against mages and magic-focused champions  

## Attack Speed

**Attack Speed** increases how fast your champion performs basic attacks.  

- Increases sustained damage over time  
- Especially important for marksmen  

## Ability Haste

**Ability Haste** reduces the cooldowns of your abilities.  

- Allows abilities to be used more often  
- Important for champions that rely on spells
