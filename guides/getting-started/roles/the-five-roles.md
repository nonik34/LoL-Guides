# The Five Roles

There are **five main roles** in League of Legends. Each has a unique position
on the map and responsibilities for the team.  

### Top Lane
- A solo lane at the top of the map  
- Often played by durable fighters or tanks  
- Focuses on surviving, farming, and controlling the top side  

### Jungle
- Roams the areas between lanes  
- Kills neutral monsters for gold and experience  
- Helps lanes by ganking and securing objectives like Dragon and Baron  

### Mid Lane
- Usually a solo lane in the center of the map  
- Often played by mages or assassins  
- Focuses on dealing high damage and roaming to influence other lanes  

### Bot Lane
- Typically played by a Marksman
- Is the teams designated damage dealer  
- Focuses on farming, team fights, and staying alive

### Support
- Stays near the ADC in the bottom lane  
- Provides vision, crowd control, heals, or shields     
- Helps protect teammates and control map objectives
