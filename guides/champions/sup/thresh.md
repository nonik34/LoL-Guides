# THRESH GUIDE
## Basic Info:
Thresh has lots of skill expression making him a great champ to OTP.  
This is reinforced by his unusually low ban rate of 5.2% despite his popularity, being the one of the most picked support champions in diverse elos ranging from silver to master+.  
## Combos:
aa > e  
flash > e: keep cursor close to champion to minimise travel time  
q > r > (q2) > (e): you only need to use e and q2 if the hooked enemy is outside a certain range  
w > flash > (e): wait for teammate to click lantern, use to escape or bring jungler in for a gank  
## Q Buffer and E Cancels:  
flay can cancel a lot of movement abilities (trist w, rell w, etc.)  
ur q goes through even if ur CCd if you started channelling it >  
this means, body blocking an enemy CC or hook then channelling ur own hook (which if you hit it, procs aftershock if you have it, combined with unflinching makes u very tanky so u wont die) you can neutralize enemy engage  
## E vs Q lvl1:  
usually, e  
at level 1 if you can walk up and flay, just do it > even trade is good since lvl2 is very stong for thresh  
only do this tho if ur adc is likely to follow up (if theyre standing way back csing minions they probably wont)  
start q if enemy is giga long ranged and/or you die walking up  
since most people start e, q start can surprise enemy if you dont make it obvious you have it by standing far away from the wave  
## Minions:  
look to hook when enemy steps up to last hit a minion, since they will be locked into the aa animation for a short time (or if they dodge, they miss the cs, which laners DONT LIKE DOING, ESPECIALLY IF ITS A CANNON MINION)  
you can also do this when the enemy support trys to proc their relic stack  
minions will sometimes leave a gap between them, which you can hook through. this is good for 2 reasons:  
first, the enemy wont expect the hook coming so they wont try to dodge  
and second, even if you miss, if you hit a minion and the angle is good, you can instantly recast q and then flay (q doesnt have a recast cd if you hit a minion, so you can also q a jungle camp and recast immediately to escape)  
you can also sometimes just look to q a minion, recast and flay > only do this if you win the fight 100% despite hooking basically no one  
dont forget, going for unlikely q angles is not bad if you cant get punished for it, worst case scenario you miss and walk back until you have q again  
## Mind Games:  
hook is super easy to hit if enemies are: hugging a wall; or hugging eachother (since 2 people dodging a hook is less likely than one, and they bodyblock eachother)  
misdirection: walk one way, hook the other  
when chasing someone and you need to lang a hook, click in front of them then hook behind (without issuing a movement command)  
this will make them think ur hooking in front of them and turn back walking straight into ur hook  
over the course of a game, try to mix up where you throw ur hooks  
usually the same trick will only work twice against the same player *(fool me once, shame on you; fool me twice, shame on me; fool me three times, now thats just straight BS)*  
## Flash Q:  
you usually dont want to flash q since its very low percentage play, and the flash animation alerts the enemy a hook is coming  
but if you do, try to flash as close to them as possible, so you have the largest angle possible to hit them (flash q at max range is a 1% play, only works if you know enemy has no movement abilities, no flash and is hugging a wall > dont do it)  
if you want to precict a movement ability or a flash, do it perpendicular to ur original hook direction > enemies usually flash sideways, not backwards  
also, if ur close to someone and are 99% likely to hit anyway, try to not hook straight at them, but target someone behind them > in the unlikely scenario the first target dodges ur hook, u hit the second one 100% since they arent expecting it  
## After you hit a Q:  
you can generally do one of three things:  
nothing, as if ur just poking with q > do this if you cant kill the enemy u just hooked and theyre stronger than you so if you fight u die  
extend the trade with e > do this if you cant kill the target but want to fight since ur at no risk of dying  
or take the ride to them to all in if ur sure you can kill  
usually, isolating a single target by not taking q2 is better than throwing yourself into the enemy team, because that isolates YOU form your team  
if u DONT TAKE HOOK INSTANTLY but wait out the full cc duration and take the hook at the end, during your travel time the enemy you hooked can use movement abilities or flash do reposition where you will end up > they can flash you under turret or into their team  
this means, you have to be very desicive on what you do, and very quick on desicion making  
## Lantern:  
if you lantern to save someone, you need to cover the most distance with it > throw lantern and walk back  
ur w timing matters a lot > if you throw it out while ur teammate is CCd (they cant take it) u give the enemy time to bodyblock it for ur teammate  
throw lantern where your teammate wants to go, and where there is no clutter that blocks it  
ur w has a collision box. > you can w between the enemy turret and wall on botlane which will make them autopath around the turret if they click behind it giving you a free hook angle  
## The Priority Target:  
as a hook champion, thresh needs to know who to target  
usually the enemy carry, or whomever is most fed  
if ur target is already basically dead, dont try to hit them more than once (e passive)
position and look for the next target or zone the enemy from helping instead
